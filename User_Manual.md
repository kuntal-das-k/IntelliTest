## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account / Logging In
1. Navigate to the **IntelliTest Home Page**.
2. Click on the **Try Free** or **Login** button.
3. You will be redirected to the secure Google Login page.
4. Select your Google account to sign in. No additional passwords to remember!
5. *New users automatically receive 10 free credits to try out the platform.*

---

## 2. Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, you will be taken to your **Dashboard**. Here you can find:

- **Credit Balance**: Shows your remaining credits or if you are on an Unlimited plan. Each paper generation costs 5 credits.
- **Current Plan**: Displays whether you are on the Free plan or a premium tier.
- **Papers Generated**: The total number of exams you have created.
- **Redeem Coupon**: A section to enter promotional or purchase codes to add credits or activate unlimited plans.
- **Past Papers History**: A list of previously generated papers. You can click on any past paper to view it again.

## 3. Generating an Exam Paper

To create a new exam paper, click the **✨ Generate New Paper** button on your dashboard. This will cost 5 credits.

### Step-by-Step Form:
1. **Board**: Select the educational board (e.g., WBBSE, WBCHSE, JEE Mains, JEE Advanced, WBJEE).
2. **Class**: Choose the appropriate class. Options will update based on the selected Board.
3. **Subject**: Currently locked to **Mathematics**.
4. **Chapter**: Type the chapter name (e.g., *Algebra*, *Calculus*, *Trigonometry*).
5. **Question Types**: Check the boxes for the types of questions you want (e.g., *Multiple Choice*, *Short Answer*, *Long Answer*).
6. **Questions per Type**: Specify how many questions of each selected type you want.
7. **Difficulty**: Use the slider to set the difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard).
8. **Total Marks**: Enter the maximum marks for the paper (e.g., 50, 100).
9. **Time Duration**: Select the exam duration from the dropdown (e.g., 2 hours, 3 hours).
10. **School/Institute Name** (Optional): Add your institution's name to appear in the header.
11. **Exam Date** (Optional): Set the date of the exam.

Once all fields are filled, click **✨ Generate Paper (5 credits)**. The AI will craft your paper in 15-30 seconds.

---

## 4. Previewing and Editing Papers

After generation, you are taken to the **Paper Preview** page. 

- **Reviewing Questions**: Scroll through the generated sections to review the math questions. The questions are formatted properly with mathematical symbols.
- **Regenerate Single Question**: If you don't like a specific question, click the small "Regenerate" button next to it. The AI will replace it with a new question of the same type and difficulty. *(Note: Regenerating a single question does not cost extra credits).*

---

## 5. Downloading as PDF

Once you are satisfied with the paper:
1. Look for the **Download PDF** button at the top or bottom of the preview page.
2. Click it, and the system will instantly process the paper into a professional, printable PDF document.
3. The PDF will feature your School Name (if provided), proper formatting, and clear sections ready for distribution to your students.

---

## 6. Managing Credits & Upgrading

If you run out of credits, you can easily top up or upgrade:
1. Go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Click on **Upgrade Plan**.
3. Choose a plan that suits your needs (e.g., Starter, Pro, Unlimited).
4. After purchasing, you will receive a **Coupon Code** from the administrator.
5. Enter this code in the **🎟️ Redeem Coupon** box on your Dashboard to activate your new credits or unlimited access.
